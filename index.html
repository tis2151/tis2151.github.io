Faculty of Computing and Informatics is an important academic unit of the Multimedia University, designed to serve the Multimedia Super Corridor (MSC) and the wider community by creating diverse educational opportunities in IT and multimedia. The faculty trains and produces IT professionals of very high calibre through our world class facilities for learning and research in IT, highly qualified staff and collaborative efforts with R&D groups and industries within the MSC.
