Postgraduate Programmes by Coursework
Master of Computer Science (M.Comp.Sc.) in Software Engineering & Software Architecture

Multimedia University is committed to produce and train quality IT workers to help the country to propel into the information age. To accelerate this production and meet the unprecedented demand for multimedia and IT professionals, Multimedia University offers a Master’s degree in Computer Science (M.CS) specializing in Software Engineering and Software Architecture (SESA). This course is targeted at IT and computer science graduates and aims to produce qualified IT professionals who specialize in software engineering and software architecture. The world is moving towards a knowledge economy where information grows at a fast speed. The number of large-scale software projects require well-trained personnel to design and manage is increasing. Furthermore, managers in business organisations need good information and assistance to make critical decisions from factual data. This creates a need to train qualified professionals to fulfill this demand. Our objectives are to produce:

  • software professionals who are well-versed in advanced theories and practices in the architecture and engineering of large-scale and long-term software systems.
  • analytics professionals who have the ability to perform data analysis and use analytics to manage today's business environment.
  • leaders, critical thinkers and technopreneurs in IT for knowledge economy.
