Subject ID Subject Name
TGD3251
3D Game Programming

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To equip students with 3D game programming and development within the PC/Windows environment.

  • Justification for including the course in the programme :

    To provide student with the skills and techniques to develop 3D games using appropriate tools.
TIS3351
Advance Database

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To strengthen students' understanding on database systems and provide broader aspects of management (data mining and warehousing) and development of databases.

  • Justification for including the course in the programme :

    To prepare graduates to understand, plan, design, implement and manage various kinds of databases ranging from traditional databases to XML databases
TCP2101
Algorithm Design and Analysis

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To introduce algorithm design techniques through the study of algorithms from various design paradigms.

  • Justification for including the course in the programme :

    To provide students with good analytical and design skills in computer algorithm.
TIC3151
Artificial Intelligence

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To equip students with principles and methods in artificial intelligence such as search techniques, logic, knowledge representation techniques and alternate ways of representing knowledge as well as explore the consequences of the various representations in AI problem solving.

  • Justification for including the course in the programme :

    To provide students with AI knowledge of search, logic and various knowledge representations for AI problem solving.
TMA1101
Calculus

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To equip students with various essential mathematical concepts and analytical techniques for problem solving.

  • Justification for including the course in the programme :

    To provide students with basic mathematical skills for use in subsequent courses.
TMA1301
Computational Methods

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To equip students with knowledge of computational methods and ability to manipulate software in solving mathematical problems.

  • Justification for including the course in the programme :

    To provide students with general computational methods knowledge and problem solving skills using software.
TSN1101
Computer Architecture and Organization

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To provide the knowledge of how the data can be represented and how the different arithmetic operations can be done using different types of number systems and codes.
TGD2151
Computer Graphics Fundamentals

  • Objective of the course in the programme :

    To equip students with program writing skill from a practical specification and produce realistic graphics output

  • Justification for including the course in the programme :

    To provide students with knowledge on graphics pipeline, graphic rendering, interactive and real-time graphics development and technical programming skill to produce realistic and smooth graphics output.
